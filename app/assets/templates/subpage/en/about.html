CLASS FOR BEST EDUCATION (CFBE)

Empowering high-school youth one special English language class at a time.

CFBE is a project funded by a United States Department of State grant and managed by The Kings Foundation, a NGO helping Polish students apply to top universities abroad. CFBE will reach pupils from rural and urban areas of Poland. It is designed to empower high-school youth during English language classes. We achieve it through:

  • providing access to knowledge about higher education programmes, application system in the US,
  • discussing future opportunities.

CFBE team specially designed a module that teachers will implement within high-school English language curriculum. Through this they will equip their students with knowledge about higher education opportunities abroad.
